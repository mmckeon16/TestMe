About The Tool

Here is what it does.

This tool is a SSW-215 Individual Software Engineering Course project by Mary McKeon, Kyle Gensheimer, and Sabrina Motiwala. We created this resource for students at stevens to have an open place to upload any test resources they need, and to give back to other students by posting their past tests or class resources

Each resource has a rating that can be completed by the users of the system. If the rating is below 3 stars for 5 reviews, it is removed from the service to keep the resources relevant.

You can report any problems with the systems or recommendations to the contact page!
